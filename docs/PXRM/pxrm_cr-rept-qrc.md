| ####### Clinical Reminders Reports  Reports can be created by the following criteria:  - Individual Patient - Hospital Location:  Future appointment/ past encounter by clinic or stop code: inpatients by current inpatients or by admission date - OE/RR Team (all patients in team) - PCMM Provider (all practitioner patients, either all patients or those with PC assignment only) - PCMM Team (all patients in team)  A SUMMARY report displays totals of how many patients of those selected have reminders due or applicable.  *The SUMMARY report may include several reminders.*  *A detailed report displays patients with reminders due in alphabetical order. The report displays for each patient the date the reminder is due, the date the reminder was last done, and the next appointment date. The detailed report can also list all future appointments.
